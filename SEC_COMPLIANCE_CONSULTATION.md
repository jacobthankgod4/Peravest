# PeraVest SEC Compliance & Cooperative License Language Consultation

## Executive Summary
This document provides atomic guidance on rewording website content to comply with cooperative license regulations and avoid SEC scrutiny. The key principle: **shift from "investment" language to "membership participation" and "cooperative benefits"**.

---

## 1. CRITICAL TERMINOLOGY CHANGES

### ❌ AVOID (SEC-Regulated Language)
- "Investment" / "Invest"
- "Returns" / "ROI" / "Yield"
- "Securities" / "Shares"
- "Guaranteed returns"
- "Profit sharing"
- "Capital appreciation"
- "Crowdfunding"
- "Raise capital"

### ✅ USE (Cooperative-Compliant Language)
- "Membership participation"
- "Member benefits"
- "Cooperative dividends"
- "Member units" / "Participation units"
- "Projected member benefits"
- "Cooperative surplus distribution"
- "Property participation"
- "Cooperative funding"

## 3. CRITICAL DISCLAIMERS TO ADD

### Add to Every Page with Participation Offers:

```
⚠️ IMPORTANT COOPERATIVE NOTICE
PeraVest is a cooperative platform. Member participations are NOT investments 
and are NOT regulated by the SEC. Members participate in real estate projects 
through a cooperative model. Member benefits depend on project performance and 
cooperative operations and are NOT guaranteed.

This is NOT a securities offering. Members should understand that:
- Cooperative participations carry risk
- Past performance does not guarantee future benefits
- Member benefits are subject to project performance
- Cooperative operations may affect benefit distribution
```

## 13. FINAL NOTES

1. **Consistency is Key**: Use the same terminology across ALL platforms (web, mobile, emails, admin)
2. **Transparency**: Always disclose the cooperative nature and lack of SEC regulation
3. **Risk Disclosure**: Be clear that member benefits are NOT guaranteed
4. **Member Rights**: Emphasize cooperative membership benefits and voting rights
5. **Legal Review**: Have a lawyer review all changes before implementation
6. **Documentation**: Keep records of all language changes for compliance
